About

Born on the frozen tundra of Northern Michigan, I spent many years wandering the earth. During this time I heard, told, and took part in many tall tales before sailing to the East. In short time I had discovered the continent of Australia and used the opportunity to open the very first Blockbuster Video franchise in Sydney.

The call of the road was strong, however, and I soon made my way to other ports and other cities. After a long successful career as a chimney sweep I made my fortune by inventing Post-It notes. Feeling I had more to give back to the world, I decided to learn C#.
